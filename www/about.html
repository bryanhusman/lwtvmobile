About Us

LoveWorld TV is a UK based Christian television station at the forefront of Christian TV programming with the objective to establish a medium by which diverse needs of varying groups of people, organization and individuals can be identified and catered for through broadcast programming.

LOVEWORLD Television has a mandate inspired by the Holy Spirit of God to take the divine presence of our Lord Jesus Christ to the people of the world, demonstrating the character of His Spirit.

Hence, we aim to provide life changing, informative and highly inspiring motivational programs to a broad viewing audience spanning across the United Kingdom, Europe, and U.S.A and beyond, via our satellite network and internet online streaming.

LOVEWORLD Television has however emerged into an undisputable vibrant satellite TV station, known around the world of TV industry, with an immerse viewership audience.
